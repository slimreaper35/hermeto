# Hermeto Code of Conduct

By contributing to Hermeto in any form (via submitting a pull request,
creating an issue, discussion or participating in any conversation in
the project repository) one agrees to abide by the principles outlined
in this document.

1. Adhere to [contributor covenant][] Code of Conduct which is applicable
   word for word to this project;
1. Adhere to [contribution guidelines][];
1. Adhere to [AI contribution policy][];
1. Be respectful to peers in choice of language and in choice of
   topics to bring up; remember that the project has limited resources and
   plans for years to come, these resources cannot be spent at will;
1. Do not generate noise in the project, AI-assisted or hand-crafted: low-effort
   submissions for the sake of submitting will be treated as such and can lead
   to a ban.

Whether some activity violates the code of conduct is ultimately decided
by project maintainers who will issue a warning or, if warnings are not heeded,
ban a violating contributor from participation for a period of time chosen
at their discretion. A violation could be reported using standard GitHub mechanisms.
A maintainer will act as an arbiter in case any concerns
of code of conduct breach arise.

[contributor covenant]: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/0/code_of_conduct/
